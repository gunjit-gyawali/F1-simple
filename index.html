Formula 1 (F1)

Formula One (F1) is the top level of car racing where the fastest, most advanced cars compete on tracks all over the world. Drivers race for teams and try to score points in each race to win the championship by the end of the season. It's not just about driving fast though things like strategy, pit stops, tire choices, and even weather can totally change the race. The cars are super high-tech and insanely quick, which makes every race exciting and sometimes unpredictable, so it's really fun to watch.
